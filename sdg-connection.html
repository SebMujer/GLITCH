SDG 5: GENDER EQUALITY

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls

CONNECTION TO SDG 5
This poster connects to SDG 5: Gender Equality by showing that people should not be judged based on gender or stereotypes. It reminds us that everyone deserves equal respect, rights, and chances in life. The image shows that even if people look or express themselves differently, they are still equal and should be accepted. Overall, the poster supports SDG 5 by promoting understanding, inclusivity, and fairness for all genders.

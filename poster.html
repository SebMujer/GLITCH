"Break the bias, build equality"
By: Franco Salanguit
This poster blends two identities into one, breaking the idea that gender must be separate or fixed. The soft, overlapping colors create a sense of unity and harmony, showing that differences don’t have to divide us. Its message, “we’re not different at all,” reminds viewers that gender doesn’t define a person’s value or potential. At our core, we share the same humanity, and true equality comes from accepting and embracing that.

SDG 5: GENDER EQUALITY

Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls
